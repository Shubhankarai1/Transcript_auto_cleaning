Topic: Common Risks in AI Systems and Implementing AI Governance according to ISO 42001
Chunk: 2

Explanation:
The lecture discusses common risks associated with AI systems, including bias, hallucination, and data leakage/privacy. It also explains how the international standard ISO 42001 can be used to implement AI governance in a company to mitigate these risks.

Key Points:
- Bias: An AI system treating one group of people differently or preferentially over another group, which is ethically wrong and bad for the company.
- Hallucination: An AI system providing incorrect answers convincingly, making it difficult to know whether the answer is right or wrong.
- Data Leakage/Privacy: Sensitive data being used for training an AI model without proper controls, potentially leading to privacy breaches.
- ISO 42001: An international standard explaining how to implement AI governance, accepted in all countries and offering certification for companies that adhere to its guidelines.
