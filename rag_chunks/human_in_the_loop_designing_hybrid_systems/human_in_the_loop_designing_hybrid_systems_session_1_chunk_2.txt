Topic: Human-in-the-Loop in AI Systems
Chunk: 2

Explanation: The lecture discusses the importance of integrating human judgment into AI systems, especially in high-stake decisions, irreversible actions, regulatory compliance, and MCP server integration. The speaker also explains how to gradually remove human intervention as trust is built between humans and the AI system.

Key Points:
1. High-stake decisions require human intervention.
2. Irreversible actions need human oversight.
3. Regulatory compliance necessitates human oversight.
4. MCP server integration requires human judgment.
5. Sending responses to customers needs human oversight.
6. Trust building is essential in AI systems.
7. Human-in-the-loop can be avoided when confidence in the system's performance is high.
8. Progressive autonomy allows starting with human control and gradually moving towards full autonomy as trust is built.
9. MCP provides elicitations for building human-in-the-loop aspects.
10. Step functions have wait for callback capabilities for approvals or task tokens.
11. Durable functions perform similar work as step functions in Lambda.
12. LangGraph tools can change the state behind the scenes if a human is in place.
