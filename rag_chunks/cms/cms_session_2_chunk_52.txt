Explanation: The lecture discusses the role of routers and orchestration in an agent. Routers are decision-making agents that classify a query into multiple grounds such as rack, analysis, reasoning, history, etc. They process the query and customize it for user intent. Orchestration is a platform that sends queries to other platforms like NAN.

Key Points:
1. Routers are decision-making agents that process queries and customize them for user intent.
2. Orchestration is a platform that connects multiple other things by sending queries to other platforms.
3. Routers and orchestration play crucial roles in an agent's functioning.
