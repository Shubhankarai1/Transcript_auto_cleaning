Topic: Distinguishing User Messages from System Messages in AI Interaction
Chunk: 14

Explanation: The lecture explains the importance of maintaining history and context to help an AI model differentiate between user messages and system messages in a conversation. The system keeps track of this history by stacking the messages in order, with the first message being the user's and the second being the system's. This allows the AI to understand the sequence of the conversation.

Key Points:
- Maintaining history is crucial for AI to differentiate between user and system messages.
- The AI uses the order of the messages to understand the sequence of the conversation.
- The sequence is important as it helps the AI to respond appropriately.
