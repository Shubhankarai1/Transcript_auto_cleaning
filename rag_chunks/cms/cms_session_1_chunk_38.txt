Explanation:
The lecture discussed Language Models (LLMs) which are probabilistic models used to predict words based on a dictionary. The topmost probability is not selected for output, instead, algorithms such as Top P, Top K, and temperature come into play to select a probabilistic value. The teacher mentioned that these concepts were covered in previous sections and will be explained further in future sessions.

Key Points:
- LLMs predict words based on a dictionary
- Top P, Top K, and temperature are used to select probabilistic values for output
- Raising the temperature makes the output more random while reducing it makes it less random and more deterministic
