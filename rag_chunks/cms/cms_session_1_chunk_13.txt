Topic: Understanding AI and LLM Communication
Chunk: 13

Explanation: The lecture discusses the basic structure of communication between a human user and an LLM (Large Language Model). It explains that the communication consists of three types of messages: System Message, Human Message, and AI Message (the output from the AI). The key point is to clearly differentiate these messages to ensure the AI understands the user's query.

Key Points:
- Communication between human and LLM consists of system message, human message, and AI message.
- System message is given by the user to define the context (identity of the AI).
- Human message is the user's question or request.
- AI message is the output from the AI in response to the human message.
- Proper differentiation of these messages helps the AI understand the user's query better.
