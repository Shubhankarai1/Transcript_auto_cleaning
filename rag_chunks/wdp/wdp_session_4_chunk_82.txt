Student Doubts:
1. Where does the output go when it's returned as a dictionary?
   - The output will be added to the actual output, which is given to the LLM to reply.
2. What happens when the output comes in?
   - Once the output comes, you classify it and give it to the LLM to reply.
3. Why is `pyantic` used with LLMs?
   - `pyantic` is used because it ensures that the output will always be in a particular format, which can be added to the state for further processing by the next LLM.
