Explanation: The discussion revolves around training agents (AI models) to improve their performance based on feedback from past conversations. The two methods discussed are RAgs and Knowledge Graphs.

Key Points:
- RAgs can be used to store initial conversations, compare the responses given by AI with previous bad responses, and encourage improvement in the agent's responses.
- Retraining is essential for improving the agent over time.
- Knowledge graphs can be utilized to store attributes of a person, such as preferences or dislikes, and present them to the AI during conversations to personalize interactions.
