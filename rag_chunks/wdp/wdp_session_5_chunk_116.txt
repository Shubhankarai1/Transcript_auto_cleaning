Topic: Workflow and Patterns in Communication
Chunk: 116

Explanation:
The lecture discusses the concept of workflow and patterns in communication within a system. The process is defined as a workflow, which is a business journey being solved. The focus is on how communication occurs between different agents (specialists) in various patterns.

Key Points:
- Workflow: A business journey being solved
- Patterns: Multi-agent patterns such as supervisor-peer and hubspoke topologies
- Communication Establishment: Between agents within a pattern, which can involve routing queries and calls between the agents (supervisor, policy specialist, calculation specialist)
