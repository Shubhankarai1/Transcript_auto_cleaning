Explanation: The professor explains that real life projects do not have clear criteria to judge the quality of a solution, unlike school or college projects where marks are given based on solutions. Key Points:
- Real life projects lack clear criteria for judging solutions
- Solutions are based on one's understanding of the problem
- Feedback is important to improve solutions
- Projects are an endless process of designing, discussing, and redesigning
